The What:

This search tool is designed to help you decide on a dog breed. The search feature below allows you to quickly compare two dog breeds and their dog breed facts. The side by side comparison is there to make the decision making process more efficient.

So why should you use this tool?
  • To compare two breeds
  • To learn some fun facts
  • To see photos of adorable dogs

The How:

Please enter two dog breeds in the search bars below. Once you have entered those, hit the search button and watch the tables populate with your chosen breeds. In order to look at a different breed, hit the reset button next to the search bar.
